What Is Our Iron Removal Filtration?

Our iron removal filters are specially designed to remove the excess iron contant present in the feed water with minimum pressure drop. Most iron removal filtration systems operate on the principal of oxidizing on the principal of oxidizing the iron to convert from a Ferrous (Fe++/Soluble) to Ferric (Fe+++/ Insoluble) state.


Iron Removal Filtration?

Iron removal Filters can be based on different filtration media, depending on the iron and manganese concentration, O2 level and Co2 content and hardness of water.
